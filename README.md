# Google-Advanced-Data-Analytics-Cert-Capstone-Project
This capstone project was focused on accurately predicting turn over for a company named "Salifort Motors". The company had been experiencing significant turnover and wanted to utilize employee data to determine what are some of the driving forces that are causing the turnover. I conducted EDA to obtain a better understanding of the data and also to determine what would be the best way to accurately predict which employees are likely to leave the company. For the sake of leveraging what I have learned in this course I created 2 different way of predicting turnover: Modeling Approach A: Logistic Regression Model. Modeling Approach B: Tree-based Model.
